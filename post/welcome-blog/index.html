Hello World!

Hi everyone, I'm Khang.

I am a Cybersecurity fresher on a journey to become an IT Security Officer, and eventually a Cyber Security Consultant (ambitious, I know!). Currently, I act as a penetration tester, a red teamer, a bounty hunter, and a self-proclaimed ethical hacker. I am a big fan of new technologies, but generally, I'm just an ordinary man.

Welcome to Dulkanggg's Corner, my personal space on the internet.

What is this place?

This website is where I archive interesting news, articles, tutorials, and links that I find useful or intriguing. The content here mainly revolves around:

  • Cybersecurity: Write-ups, CTF solutions, and security concepts.
  • IT & Tech: Networking, Linux, and setting up local environments.
  • My Projects: Updates on things I'm building (like e-commerce platforms or security tools).

I hope you will find useful information on my website.

Feel free to connect with me on social media or drop me an email. I usually respond within a few days.
